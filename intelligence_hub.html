Intelligence Hub

Cross-source queries, alerts, and the loop closure into TBG's RFQ business
What this will do

The Intelligence Hub is where the five input modules combine. Each module alone is a data source; the Hub is where you find competitive intelligence and translate it into sourcing action.

Brand and product unification happens here — same product across factory announcements, online catalog, social mentions, news coverage, and physical shop-outs all rolls up to one canonical "observed product" record, even when spelled differently across sources.

The cross-source queries that matter
  • Factory announcements from 6+ months ago that haven't appeared in retail yet — TBG opportunities
  • Products trending on TikTok this month also stocked at Burlington in shop-outs — bridges social to shelf
  • Items with biggest price gap between off-price in-store and full-price online — sourcing arbitrage
  • Factories whose announcements consistently hit retail within 90 days — reliable indicators vs vaporware
  • Products spotted in retail whose factory of origin TBG doesn't know yet — supplier discovery into RFQs
  • MFG vendor codes appearing across multiple brand identities — same supplier, multiple brand fronts
  • Brands that surged in social mentions 6 months ago, now on shelf at 3+ retailers — pattern validation
