Social Trends

Trend signal capture from TikTok and Instagram
What this will do

Weekly Apify scrapes of top posts per monitored hashtag across TikTok and Instagram. For each post, Claude extracts mentioned brands, products, ingredients, trend descriptors (slugging, glass skin, barrier repair), sentiment, haul-video flag, retailer name-checks, and category mapping into TBG taxonomy.

Observations roll up into rolling trend records with velocity metrics, so you can see what's rising, peaking, or declining over time.

What gets tracked
MetricDescription
VelocityWeek-over-week mention change per topic / brand / ingredient
ReachTotal view count across captured observations
Lifecycle stageEmerging / Rising / Peak / Declining (heuristic)
Unique creatorsOrganic spread vs single-influencer push
Sample monitored hashtags

Beauty / HBC seeds: #cleanbeauty, #skincareproducts, #turmericmask, #glassskin, #beautyfinds, #drugstoremakeup. Retailer-specific: #tjmaxxhaul, #burlingtonfinds, #targetbeauty. Brand-specific tags added as TBG identifies brands of interest.

Cross-source queries this enables
  • Products trending on TikTok this month that are also stocked at Burlington in shop-outs — demand confirmed on shelf
  • Ingredients gaining momentum across creators — formulation roadmap for upcoming RFQs
  • Brands surging in social mentions 6 months ago now on shelf at 3+ retailers — validation that social signal predicts retail
  • Retailers being name-checked in haul videos — competitive shelf attention
