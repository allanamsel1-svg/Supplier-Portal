News & Editorial

Editorial signal capture across six tiers of publication
What this will do

Most beauty and HBC publications publish RSS feeds. The platform polls them daily, sends each new article through Claude for entity extraction, and stores structured mentions of brands, products, ingredients, retailers, and factories — linked into the same cross-source canonical registry as the other intelligence modules.

Result: a queryable, time-stamped feed of every relevant mention across the editorial landscape, automatically tied to TBG's existing brand, product, and factory records.

Why celebrity press despite low authority weight

Authority weight measures factual reliability. Celebrity press carries a different signal: mass consumer demand. When People features an $8 drugstore mascara "selling out everywhere," that product will appear in TBG's shop-outs within weeks. Leading indicator of shelf demand — especially relevant for HBC sourcing that skews drugstore and mass tier.

Cross-source queries this enables
  • Brands mentioned in Beauty Independent in last 30 days that are also in TBG's factory pipeline — sourcing opportunities
  • Ingredients trending up over 6 months in editorial coverage — formulation roadmap
  • Trends spotted in factory intel that later get editorial validation — confirmation signal
  • M&A and supplier consolidation affecting TBG's competitive set or supply base
  • Brand crises (recalls, ingredient controversies) on competitors TBG sources against — opportunistic positioning
