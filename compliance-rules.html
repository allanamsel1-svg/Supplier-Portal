Compliance Rules by Category
Define which factory certifications are required vs preferred for each product category. The compliance engine uses these to flag factories and gate RFQs.
How this works: For each category, mark each cert as Required (factory must have a current one to qualify), Preferred (nice-to-have, flagged but not blocking), or N/A. A factory missing a required cert is flagged but never deleted — they can get certified later and auto-qualify. RFQs in a category inherit these requirements as the baseline.
